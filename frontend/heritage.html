North Kanara · South Kanara · Mumbai · San Francisco Bay

One family table, four beloved shores.

Rupa preserves the old coastal grammar: ghee roasts, spice pastes, patient tempering, and Amchi kitchen notes carried down from the Konkan coast — Mangalore, Udupi, and the kitchens of Mumbai.

Ruchi carries the same care into a modern pantry: layered cakes, matcha, coffees, soda floats, and celebratory drinks made for today's gatherings in the San Francisco Bay.

Together they keep one family table set across four beloved shores — every recipe a place, a memory, and a little ceremony.
